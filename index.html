Cat Trivia - See how much you know!!

Welcome to cat trivia. You have 15 seconds to complete each question and if the question is blank when the 15 seconds are up, it will be counted as an incorrect answer. If no answers are clicked throughout the game, then nothing will happen. The game will then move on to the next question. Your score will be displayed at the end and you will be prompted to enter a y to play again or a n to stop play.
